.. py:class:: WbulkLung(Phis, Phis0, parameters)

   Bases: :py:obj:`dolfin_mech.materials.elastic.Elastic.ElasticMaterial`


   Class representing the bulk (volumetric) elastic energy contribution specifically tailored for lung tissue.

   This material model uses a logarithmic energy potential to penalize deviations
   from the reference solid volume fraction :math:`\Phi_{s0}`.

   The strain energy density function is defined as:

   .. math::
       \Psi = \kappa \left( \frac{\Phi_s}{\Phi_{s0}} - 1 - \ln\left( \frac{\Phi_s}{\Phi_{s0}} \right) \right)

   Where:
       - :math:`\kappa` is the bulk modulus (penalty parameter).
       - :math:`\Phi_s` is the current solid volume fraction.
       - :math:`\Phi_{s0}` is the reference solid volume fraction.

   :ivar kappa: Bulk modulus parameter.
   :vartype kappa: :py:class:`dolfin.Constant`
   :ivar Psi: The calculated strain energy density.
   :vartype Psi: :py:class:`UFL expression`
   :ivar dWbulkdPhis: The derivative of the energy with respect to
                      the solid volume fraction :math:`\frac{\partial \Psi}{\partial \Phi_s}`.

   :vartype dWbulkdPhis: :py:class:`UFL expression`
