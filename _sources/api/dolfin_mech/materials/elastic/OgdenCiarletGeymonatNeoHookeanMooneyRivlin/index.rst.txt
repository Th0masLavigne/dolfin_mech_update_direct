   Class representing a composite hyperelastic material model.

   Combines Ciarlet-Geymonat volumetric energy with a combined Neo-Hookean and
   Mooney-Rivlin deviatoric response.

   This class serves as a high-level wrapper that allows for sophisticated
   material modeling where the volumetric behavior (compressibility) and
   the shear behavior (based on the first and second invariants) are
   independently controlled but additively combined.

   The total strain energy density is:

   .. math::
       \Psi_{total} = \Psi_{bulk}^{CG}(J) + \Psi_{dev}^{NH}(\mathbf{C}) + \Psi_{dev}^{MR}(\mathbf{C})

   Where:
       - :math:`\Psi_{bulk}^{CG}` is handled by :class:`OgdenCiarletGeymonatElasticMaterial`.
       - :math:`\Psi_{dev}^{NH+MR}` is handled by :class:`NeoHookeanMooneyRivlinElasticMaterial`.

   This class also provides auxiliary mechanical quantities such as the
   hydrostatic pressure and the Von Mises equivalent stress.

   :ivar kinematics: Kinematic variables and deformation tensors.
   :vartype kinematics: :py:class:`Kinematics`
   :ivar bulk: The volumetric component.
   :vartype bulk: :py:class:`OgdenCiarletGeymonatElasticMaterial`
   :ivar dev: The deviatoric component.
   :vartype dev: :py:class:`NeoHookeanMooneyRivlinElasticMaterial`
   :ivar Psi: Total strain energy density.
   :vartype Psi: :py:class:`UFL expression`
   :ivar Sigma: Total Second Piola-Kirchhoff stress tensor.
   :vartype Sigma: :py:class:`UFL expression`
   :ivar p_hydro: Hydrostatic pressure.
   :vartype p_hydro: :py:class:`UFL expression`
   :ivar Sigma_dev: Deviatoric part of the Second Piola-Kirchhoff stress.
   :vartype Sigma_dev: :py:class:`UFL expression`
   :ivar Sigma_VM: Von Mises equivalent stress calculated from :math:`\mathbf{\Sigma}_{dev}`.

   :vartype Sigma_VM: :py:class:`UFL expression`
