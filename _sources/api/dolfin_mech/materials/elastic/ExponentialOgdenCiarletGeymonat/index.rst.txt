.. py:class:: ExponentialOgdenCiarletGeymonat(kinematics, parameters)

   Bases: :py:obj:`dolfin_mech.materials.elastic.Elastic.ElasticMaterial`


   Class implementing an Exponential Ogden-Ciarlet-Geymonat elastic material model.

   This constitutive model provides a volumetric strain energy density function
   based on an exponential formulation. It is particularly effective for
   enforcing compressibility constraints or modeling the volumetric response
   of porous media like lung tissue[cite: 5, 17].

   The strain energy density :math:`\Psi` is defined as:

   .. math::

       \Psi = \alpha \left( e^{\gamma (J^2 - 1 - 2\ln J)} - 1 \right)

   :ivar kinematics: Kinematic quantities (F, J, C_inv, etc.).
   :vartype kinematics: :py:class:`Kinematics`
   :ivar alpha: Scaling parameter for the energy density.
   :vartype alpha: :py:class:`dolfin.Constant`
   :ivar gamma: Exponential exponent controlling the stiffening
                rate under volume change.
   :vartype gamma: :py:class:`dolfin.Constant`
   :ivar Psi: Strain energy density function.
   :vartype Psi: :py:class:`ufl.Form`
   :ivar Sigma: Second Piola-Kirchhoff stress tensor :math:`\mathbf{S}`.
   :vartype Sigma: :py:class:`ufl.Form`
   :ivar P: First Piola-Kirchhoff stress tensor :math:`\mathbf{P} = \mathbf{F}\mathbf{S}`.
   :vartype P: :py:class:`ufl.Form`
   :ivar sigma: Cauchy stress tensor :math:`\boldsymbol{\sigma} = J^{-1} \mathbf{P}\mathbf{F}^T`.

   :vartype sigma: :py:class:`ufl.Form`
