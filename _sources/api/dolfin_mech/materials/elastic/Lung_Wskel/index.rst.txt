dolfin_mech.materials.elastic.Lung_Wskel

   Lung skeletal tissue elasticity implementation.

   This module provides a composite hyperelastic model for lung parenchyma,
   combining Exponential-Ogden bulk and Neo-Hookean deviatoric components.

.. py:class:: WskelLung(kinematics, parameters)

   Bases: :py:obj:`dolfin_mech.materials.elastic.Elastic.ElasticMaterial`


   Class representing the skeletal (solid phase) elastic energy of the lung parenchyma.

   This model is a composite hyperelastic material that splits the strain energy
   density into a volumetric (bulk) part and a deviatoric (shear) part to
   capture the complex non-linear response of lung tissue.

   The total strain energy density is defined as:

   .. math::
       \Psi_{total} = \Psi_{bulk} + \Psi_{dev}

   Where:
       - :math:`\Psi_{bulk}` is modeled using an
         :class:`ExponentialOgdenCiarletGeymonatElasticMaterial`.
       - :math:`\Psi_{dev}` is modeled using a
         :class:`NeoHookeanMooneyRivlinElasticMaterial`.

   :ivar kinematics: Kinematic variables associated with the deformation.
   :vartype kinematics: :py:class:`Kinematics`
   :ivar bulk: The volumetric component of the lung skeleton elasticity.
   :vartype bulk: :py:class:`ElasticMaterial`
   :ivar dev: The deviatoric component of the lung skeleton elasticity.
   :vartype dev: :py:class:`ElasticMaterial`
   :ivar Psi: Total strain energy density.
   :vartype Psi: :py:class:`UFL expression`
   :ivar Sigma: Total Second Piola-Kirchhoff stress tensor.
   :vartype Sigma: :py:class:`UFL expression`
   :ivar P: Total First Piola-Kirchhoff stress tensor.
   :vartype P: :py:class:`UFL expression`
   :ivar sigma: Total Cauchy stress tensor.

   :vartype sigma: :py:class:`UFL expression`
