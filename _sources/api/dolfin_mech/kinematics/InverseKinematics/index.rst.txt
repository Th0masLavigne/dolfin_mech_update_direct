.. py:class:: InverseKinematics(u, u_old=None)

   Class to compute and store kinematic quantities for inverse non-linear solid mechanics.

   In the inverse formulation, the displacement field :math:`\mathbf{u}` is defined
   on the deformed (spatial) configuration. The deformation gradient :math:`\mathbf{F}`
   is computed as the inverse of the mapping from the reference to the deformed
   configuration: :math:`\mathbf{F} = (\mathbf{I} + \nabla \mathbf{u})^{-1}`.

   :Parameters: * **u** (:py:class:`dolfin.Function`) -- Current displacement field defined on the spatial mesh.
                * **u_old** (:py:class:`dolfin.Function, optional`) -- Displacement field from the previous
                  time step. Defaults to None.

   :ivar u: The current displacement field in the spatial configuration.
   :vartype u: :py:class:`dolfin.Function`
   :ivar dim: Spatial dimension.
   :vartype dim: :py:class:`int`
   :ivar I: Identity tensor of dimension ``dim``.
   :vartype I: :py:class:`dolfin.Identity`
   :ivar f: Spatial displacement gradient :math:`\mathbf{f} = \mathbf{I} + \nabla \mathbf{u}`.
   :vartype f: :py:class:`ufl.Form`
   :ivar F: Deformation gradient tensor :math:`\mathbf{F} = \mathbf{f}^{-1}`.
   :vartype F: :py:class:`ufl.Form`
   :ivar J: Determinant of the deformation gradient :math:`J = \det(\mathbf{F})`.
   :vartype J: :py:class:`ufl.Form`
   :ivar C: Right Cauchy-Green deformation tensor :math:`\mathbf{C} = \mathbf{F}^T \mathbf{F}`.
   :vartype C: :py:class:`ufl.Form`
   :ivar E: Green-Lagrange strain tensor :math:`\mathbf{E} = \frac{1}{2}(\mathbf{C} - \mathbf{I})`.
   :vartype E: :py:class:`ufl.Form`
   :ivar F_bar: Isochoric deformation gradient :math:`\bar{\mathbf{F}} = J^{-1/d} \mathbf{F}`.
   :vartype F_bar: :py:class:`ufl.Form`
   :ivar C_bar: Isochoric Right Cauchy-Green tensor :math:`\bar{\mathbf{C}} = \bar{\mathbf{F}}^T \bar{\mathbf{F}}`.

   :vartype C_bar: :py:class:`ufl.Form`
