dolfin_mech.materials.elastic.NeoHookeanMooneyRivlin

Composite Neo-Hookean/Mooney-Rivlin hyperelastic material.

This module provides a combined material model that sums the strain energy densities of the Neo-Hookean and Mooney-Rivlin models.

Module Contents

class dolfin_mech.materials.elastic.NeoHookeanMooneyRivlin.NeoHookeanMooneyRivlin(kinematics, parameters, decoup=False)

Bases: dolfin_mech.materials.elastic.Elastic.ElasticMaterial

Class representing a composite Neo-Hookean/Mooney-Rivlin hyperelastic material.

This model combines the strain energy densities of both the Neo-Hookean and Mooney-Rivlin models. By summing these potentials, the material response can be tuned using both the first invariant \(I_C\) (via Neo-Hookean) and the second invariant \(II_C\) (via Mooney-Rivlin).

The total strain energy density is defined as:

\[\Psi_{total} = \Psi_{NH}(C_1) + \Psi_{MR}(C_2)\]
Where:

  • \(\Psi_{NH}\) is the energy from NeoHookeanElasticMaterial.

  • \(\Psi_{MR}\) is the energy from MooneyRivlinElasticMaterial.

Variables:

  • kinematics (Kinematics) – Object containing kinematic variables and invariants.

  • nh (NeoHookeanElasticMaterial) – The Neo-Hookean component of the model.

  • mr (MooneyRivlinElasticMaterial) – The Mooney-Rivlin component of the model.

  • Psi (UFL expression) – Total strain energy density.

  • Sigma (UFL expression) – Total Second Piola-Kirchhoff stress tensor.

  • P (UFL expression) – Total First Piola-Kirchhoff stress tensor.

  • sigma (UFL expression) – Total Cauchy stress tensor.
